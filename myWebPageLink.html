Pasta Fresca

Ingredients (1-2 )

1 Eggs • 100 g Flour

Procedure

Put the flour on a wooden board and create a deep well in the middle of the flour. Crack the eggs into this well and start to whisk the eggs with the fork. As you whisk the eggs, begin gradually pulling in the flour from the sides of the well. Keep mixing till you finish the flour, then start mixing the dough with the hands, till it becomes nicely smooth. If the dough is too soft or sticky you can add a little bit of flour. This can happen because the size of the eggs is not standard and not all the flours are the same (the gluten content vary from one to the other). Let the dough rest for at least 30 minutes in a covered bowl or wrapped in the kitchen plastic film. Now you can create all the fresh pasta that you want!
